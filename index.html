About

My research bridges architectural engineering and AI, focusing on sensing occupants, reasoning over Brick/IFC knowledge graphs, and deploying agentic control strategies for healthy and net-zero buildings.

  • Master's candidate, School of Architecture, HIT (advised by Prof. Lei Hou).
  • Collaborating with HKUST on semantic AI for campus-scale digital twins.
  • Leading Prima-databot, a tool-augmented LLM agent that plans queries, writes code, and validates results for building data.
